# Canva Brief — Employee Card (1080×1080)

**Use for:** Single-employee feature cards (Maya, Jordan, Alex, Riley spotlight posts).

## Canvas
- 1080 × 1080 px, fill `#fbfaf6`

## Layout

### Eyebrow (top)
- x=80, y=85
- Hairline rule x=80→160, then mono text:
- `EMPLOYEE 0X / 04` — JetBrains Mono, 22px, `#8a8682`, tracking +400

### Big name (upper third)
- x=80, y=380
- **Cormorant Garamond Italic** 220px, color `#1a1a1a`, tracking -3
- Examples: *Maya* / *Jordan* / *Alex* / *Riley*

### Role label (under name)
- x=80, y=450
- JetBrains Mono 22px, color `#c08a3e` (amber), tracking +600
- `TREND & INSIGHTS ANALYST`
- `CONTENT STRATEGIST`
- `COPYWRITER`
- `CREATIVE DIRECTOR`

### Hairline divider
- 1px rule from x=80 to x=1000 at y=510
- Color: `#1a1a1a` at 16% opacity

### One-liner (middle)
- x=80, y=600
- Inter or DM Sans, 34px regular, color `#5a5856`
- 3 lines max, generous leading (~50px line height)

### Quote block (lower third)
- Hairline rule at y=800
- Above the quote, mono micro-label `SHE SAYS` / `HE SAYS` at y=850, 18px tracking +400, color `#8a8682`
- Quote text: Cormorant Garamond Italic, 44px, color `#c08a3e` (amber), starting y=930
- Always wrapped in real curly quotes "…"

### Footer
- Bottom-right at x=1000, y=1050
- `SOVEXA.AI`, JetBrains Mono 18px, color `#8a8682`, tracking +400
