# Canva Brief — Quote Card (1080×1080)

**Use for:** Single-line thought posts on IG / X. Reusable template.

## Canvas
- Size: 1080 × 1080 px
- Background fill: `#fbfaf6` (warm ivory)

## Layers (top → bottom)

### 1. Top-left eyebrow
- Position: x=80, y=80
- A 1px hairline rule from x=80 → x=160 (color `#1a1a1a` at 16% opacity)
- Text directly to the right at x=180, y=85
- Text: `DISPATCH 0XX — [TOPIC IN CAPS]`
- Font: **JetBrains Mono**, 22px, color `#8a8682`, letter-spacing 4px (in Canva: tracking +400)

### 2. Hero quote (center-left, vertically anchored at ~50% height)
- Font: **Cormorant Garamond Italic**, regular weight (400)
- Size: 130–180px depending on length
- Color: `#1a1a1a` for the body, **swap one focal noun to amber `#c08a3e`** in italic
- Line height: 1.05
- Letter spacing: -2 (tight)
- Max width: 920px (left aligned, x=80)

### 3. Hairline rule below quote
- 1px rule from x=80 to x=1000
- Color: `#1a1a1a` at 16% opacity
- Y: ~150px below quote baseline

### 4. Footer
- Bottom-right at x=1000, y=1010 (right-aligned)
- Text: `SOVEXA.AI`
- Font: **JetBrains Mono**, 20px, color `#8a8682`, letter-spacing 4px

## Don't
- No drop shadows.
- No gradients.
- No icons or illustrations. The composition is the design.
- No more than one amber word per card.

## Examples to drop in
- "You don't write the hooks. *Alex* does."
- "I haven't typed a caption in *21 days*."
- "Three hooks. *One* is the one."
- "Your strategist already drafted *next week.*"
