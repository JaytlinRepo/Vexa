# Reel 004 — *Hire.* (cinematic launch film)

**Reference:** Apple "Privacy. That's iPhone." commercials. Apple "Hello." launch films. Wordless until the close. Music does the work.

**Format:** No talking head. No voiceover. Locked-off cinematography. The product is the actor.
**Music:** Sparse upright piano, single sustained cello note. Building only at the close. Reference: Hauschka, Nils Frahm, Max Richter — slow.
**Color grade:** Warm ivory throughout. No cool tones. Long-shadow.
**Total runtime:** ~22 seconds. Six cuts. Each breathes for ~3.5s.
