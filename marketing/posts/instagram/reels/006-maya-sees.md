# Reel 006 — *Maya sees it first.* (foresight reveal)

**The Maya counterpart to Reel 005.** Riley showed the *transformation*. Maya shows the *foresight* — the moment a trend becomes visible before anyone else has it.

**Reference:** Bloomberg terminal aesthetics meet Apple "Privacy" film. Data is the actor.

**Format:** Native vertical 1080×1920. No talking head. No VO. Score is sparse and patient.
**Music:** Single sustained synth pad + one piano line. Tension, not energy. Reference: Trent Reznor / Atticus Ross's "The Social Network" score.

---

## Storyboard (20 seconds)

### 00:00 — 00:03 · The hour
Full-frame ivory. Massive Cormorant italic ink:
> *4:14 a.m.*
Held. Music starts: a single piano note, low, far away.

### 00:03 — 00:08 · The seven sources
Cut to ivory. Stacked editorial list scrolls upward slowly — seven source names typing in mono, one per ~700ms:
- `GOOGLE TRENDS`
- `NEWSAPI`
- `YOUTUBE`
- `REDDIT`
- `RSS`
- `INSTAGRAM`
- `TIKTOK`
After all seven, a hairline rule appears.
Bottom mono micro: `WHILE YOU SLEEP`

### 00:08 — 00:12 · The signal
Cut to a clean editorial chart filling the frame. The historical line moves left-to-right slowly. As it reaches "now," the dashed amber projection **bursts upward**, a confidence band fans out around it.
On-screen, top-left: `+340%`
On-screen, bottom: tiny mono `MAYA — 04:14 AM`

### 00:12 — 00:16 · The receipt
Cut to ivory. The trend report card slides into frame, hovering, soft long-shadow:
- Search term: *"morning routine"*
- +340% spike
- Correlation badge: *+4.2× engagement vs your baseline*
- Recommendation: *Run Tuesday. Lead with 5 a.m. angle.*
The viewer reads it. Two seconds. Music swells minutely.

### 00:16 — 00:19 · The closing line
Cut to ivory. Cormorant italic:
> Maya sees it *first.*
Music sustains.

### 00:19 — 00:20 · The signature
Beneath, mono: `SOVEXA.AI`
Music resolves.

---

## What makes this work
- **The hour as opener.** "4:14 a.m." sells the asymmetry — you sleep, she works. No words needed.
- **The list.** Seven sources in 5 seconds is the entire technical pitch, told as a film typography moment.
- **The chart is the reveal.** Apple shows iPhones. Sovexa shows curves. Same principle: let the object do the talking.
- **The receipt is proof.** The trend report card looks like real software. It is.
