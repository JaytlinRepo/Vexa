# TikTok 04 — *I have an analyst now.* (POV tap-through)

**Format:** First-person POV. Real iPhone holding the open Sovexa app. The viewer sees what the user sees — the demo plays out in their hand.
**Music:** Slow-burn lo-fi piano with a single confident kick at the +340% reveal.

---

## Storyboard (18 seconds)

### 00:00 — 00:02 · Hook
Phone in hand. App opens to the Maya page. Top of frame, on-screen mono caption:
> `I HIRED AN ANALYST.`

(Pause. Let the viewer see the SOVEXA · LIVE pulse and the *"Good morning. Maya is ready."* hero.)

### 00:02 — 00:05 · The setup
On-screen caption fades to:
> `SHE WATCHES 7 FEEDS WHILE I SLEEP.`
Camera holds on the right-rail stats: `7 sources · 04:14 AM · 0 reports`.

### 00:05 — 00:08 · The tap
Caption:
> `LET'S RUN HER.`
Thumb taps the amber "Run analysis" button. The button fades. The cards animate in.

### 00:08 — 00:13 · The reveal
Camera tilts down to the Forecast card. Hold on the +340% as the chart projection fans out.
Caption:
> `+340% PROJECTED REACH.`

Then tilt to the trend report. Hold on the search term and the recommendation.
Caption:
> `AND SHE TELLS ME WHAT TO POST.`

### 00:13 — 00:16 · The approve
Thumb hovers over the **Approve** button. Tap.
Caption:
> `THAT'S THE JOB.`

### 00:16 — 00:18 · Sign-off
Cut to ivory frame. Cormorant italic:
> Maya sees it *first.*
> SOVEXA.AI
