# TikTok 004 — *A day at the company.*

**Reference:** Apple "Shot on iPhone" series. Quiet observational beauty. The product is barely shown — only its outputs.

**Format:** First-person POV, locked-off shots, real ambient sound. No voiceover. On-screen italic-amber serif card-titles between shots, each held for 1.0s.
**Music:** Off. Real ambient room tone only. Optional one piano note at the close.
**Color:** Natural warm window light. Soft, real, unfiltered.

## What's intentional
- **No voice.** TikTok rewards talking, but Apple-tier rewards restraint. Trust the watcher.
- **Real ambient sound** until the final note. This is the difference between an ad and a moment.
- **The hand is the only human in the film.** The user is implied, never shown — because the watcher is being asked to *become* the user.
