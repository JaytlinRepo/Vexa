## Why the TikTok cut differs from the Reel
- **Hook is the reveal**, not the build. TikTok scrolls in 0.5s — the contrast must hit immediately.
- **Less cinematic silence.** Music starts at 0.0, not 0:14.
- **More on-screen text.** TikTok viewers often watch with sound off; the system has to be readable in captions alone.
