# Campaign 05 — *Cut by Riley.*

## Why this is the lead campaign (tied with Maya)
Riley is genuinely differentiated — not "auto-edit" but a real video-AI pipeline:
- **Beat detection** — finds the musical hits to cut on
- **Audio energy analysis** — ranks moments by sonic intensity
- **Motion curve** — tracks visual movement frame-by-frame
- **Quality curve** — scores each frame for sharpness, exposure, composition
- **Speech spans** — isolates clean dialogue
- **Subject classifier** — knows what's in the shot
- **Scene detection** — finds natural cuts
- **Keyframe extraction** — pulls the most representative still

Riley analyzes seven dimensions of your raw footage, then returns a paced, scored, ready-to-post vertical video. *That's* the hero story.

## The line
> **Cut by Riley.**

A signature, like a director credit. Lives in the lower-third of every output.

Companion lines (sparingly):
- *"You record. He directs."*
- *"Seven dimensions. One cut."*
- *"From 90 seconds to 30 — cut where it lands."*

- Calling it "AI video editing" — it's *direction*.
- Showing 30 software callouts ("subject detection!" "beat sync!"). The product does the work; the marketing trusts it.
- Comparing to CapCut. We don't need to.
