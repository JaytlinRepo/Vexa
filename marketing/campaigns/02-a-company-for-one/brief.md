# Campaign 02 — *A company. For one.*

## The big idea
Reframe the entire category. This isn't a creator tool. It's the first time in history one person can run a content company. The line is the campaign.

Reference: "Privacy. That's iPhone." — a single declaration, repeated across formats, that becomes the brand.

## The line
> **A company. For one.**

That's the headline. It runs everywhere. Out-of-home. Hero of the homepage. Final card of every Reel. Bio of the X account.

## Card variants

### A — The pure declaration (1080×1080)
- Cormorant italic, ink, large but not screaming
- "A company." on line 1
- "For one." on line 2 — the word *one* is amber italic
- That's the whole card.

### B — The flip (1080×1350 IG portrait)
- Top half: tiny mono row listing *Maya · Jordan · Alex · Riley* — all in one line, hairline below
- Bottom half: the line "A company. For one." dominating

### C — The OOH/billboard (3:1 horizontal)
- Centered. The full line in one row. Massive.
- Bottom right: SOVEXA.AI

## Companion lines (use sparingly, never on the same card as the headline)
- "What used to take a team. Now takes you."
- "Built for a market of one."
- "Hire four. Direct one."
