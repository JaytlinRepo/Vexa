# Campaign 01 — *Hire.*

## The big idea
Sovexa isn't a subscription. It's a payroll line. Each card in this series uses one verb and one name. Together they tell you what the product is in five words. Apart, each card is a conversation-stopper.

## The series

| # | Word | Sub-label | What it says |
|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | *Hire.* | SOVEXA.AI | The radical reframe. The whole campaign in one card. |
| 02 | *Plan.* | JORDAN — STRATEGIST | Meet your strategist. |
| 03 | *Write.* | ALEX — COPYWRITER | Meet your copywriter. |
| 04 | *Direct.* | RILEY — CREATIVE DIRECTOR | Meet your creative director. |
| 05 | *Approve.* | YOU — CEO | Your only job. |

## Composition rules (template A — The Word)
- Pure ivory canvas `#fbfaf6`
- Verb in **Cormorant Garamond Italic**, color amber `#c08a3e`, ~440px on a 1080 canvas, dead center
- ONE mono micro line below it, ~22px JetBrains Mono uppercase, color `#8a8682`, letter-spacing +400
- No eyebrow. No body. No hairlines. No footer URL. The mono line *is* the credit.
- The amber word breathes. ≥35% whitespace above, ≥35% below.

## Where to run
- IG square × 5 (a feed-post sequence; if posted in order they form a horizontal grid story)
- IG Story × 5 (1080×1920, same comp, word slightly smaller)
- X cards × 5 (one verb per post, posted as a 5-tweet drop)
- Out-of-home / billboard (the verb scales infinitely)

## The post copy (when shared)
